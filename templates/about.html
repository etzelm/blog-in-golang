  Wow, what a couple of years to start a career working with the cloud across industries. My name is Mitchell Etzel and I've decided to maintain this blog to help foster and share some of my experiences, in the hope that others may find them useful. I'm dedicating this website to the study of distributed systems, and cyber-security, because they're all around us and in most of the web services we use nowadays. As an industry, we are quickly discovering that the web services we use are not as secure as we once imagined them to be. One of the best goals that this blog could hope to achieve is shedding a better light on how to make distributed systems more secure in general.
